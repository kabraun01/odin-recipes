Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious deep dish Chicago style Pizza.
This recipe is sure to tickle your taste buds.
We all love buttery crunchy Chicago Style Pizza!

Please find the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Butter
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Thaw Dough
  2. Spread warmed butter on Dough
  3. Cover Dough in Cheese
  4. Cover Cheese with Sauce
  5. Add Toppings
  6. Bake at 500 degrees for 60 minutes
