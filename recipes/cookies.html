Cookie Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for delicious Sugar Cookies by Kevin
This recipe is sure to tickle your taste buds.
We all love sweet, suguary sugar cookies!

Please find the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Icing

Steps

  1. Mix Flour Butter and Sugar together
  2. Scoop 1 inch cookie bites onto lined cookie sheet
  3. Baked at 375 degress for 10 minutes
  4. Let cookies cool and spread icing on top
