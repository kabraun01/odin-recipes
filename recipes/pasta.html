Description

This is a recipe for a delicious spicy pink aauce tortelliini.
This recipe is sure to tickle your taste buds.
We all love Spicy, Pink Sauce Covered Tortellini!

Please find the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • Tortellini Pasta/Li>
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Hot Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Boil Tortellini for 5 minutes
  2. Mix In Alfredo Sauce
  3. Mix in Marinara Sauce
  4. Serve with Garlic Bread
